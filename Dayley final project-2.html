WHO AM I?   

Hi, I'm Adam! I currently live in Southeast Idaho, but I travel all over the western sates. I love Milton (John), Shakespeare (William), Macarthy (Cormac), Barry (Dave), and Swift (Jonathan). You won't see much of these authors' literary styles in my work, but they've been influential in my literary journey. I aim to jape, jest, joke, and satirize anything and everything.
